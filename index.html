About Us


CodeChef is a not-for-profit educational initiative by Directi, an Indian software company. It is a global programming community that fosters learning and friendly competition, built on top of the worldâ€™s largest competitive programming platform. We have also built a large community of problem curators.

In Vellore Institute of Technology, CodeChef-VIT is a technical chapter that helps students all over the campus to improve their coding skills. The core committee members of the chapters are divided into four areas of expertise- Technical, Projects, Management and Design. The main objective is to provide the students a platform where they can learn, share and test their knowledge. CodeChef- VIT keeps conducting several workshops and events in order to help the students do the same.
